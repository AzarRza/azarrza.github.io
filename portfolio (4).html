Smart Customer & Buffet Monitoring System

Course: ML2303 Digitalisation for Sustainable Production · KTH Royal Institute of Technology
Hospitaliy · IoT · Computer Vision · Cyber-Physical Systems
Overview

Developed a smart monitoring system for buffet-style restaurant operations to improve operational visibility, reduce food waste, and enhance service efficiency by applying Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) and Industrial IoT principles. The solution integrated three interconnected modules: Table Management using ultrasonic sensors, Queue Monitoring via camera-based pose estimation, and Buffet Monitoring through image-based food level detection combined with temperature and humidity sensing.

My Role: Cyber-Physical Systems & Data Developer
  • Designed and implemented the buffet monitoring logic
  • Developed a CNN-based food level classification model
  • Trained the model on 800+ labeled images to classify trays as Full, Medium, Low, or Empty
  • Built real-time image processing workflows on Raspberry Pi
  • Integrated Arduino, Raspberry Pi, environmental sensors, and Node-RED into a unified system
  • Supported end-to-end system testing and validation
