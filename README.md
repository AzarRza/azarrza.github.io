# azarrza.github.io

Hey, welcome to the repo behind my personal portfolio : [azarrza.github.io](https://azarrza.github.io).

It's a single-page site I put together to give people a real sense of who I am, what I've worked on, and what I care about,  beyond just a CV. No frameworks, no build tools, just one HTML file with everything in it.

---

## What's on the site

**Experience**:  my work history across data analytics, BI development, and teaching roles at places like KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Chapman Freeborn Airchartering, and Yapi Kredi Bank Azerbaijan, among others.

**Education**: MSc in Sustainable Production Development at KTH (Stockholm), a Master's in Business & Social Sciences from Koblenz University of Applied Sciences (Germany), and a Bachelor's in IT from Azerbaijan Technical University. KTH on a Swedish Institute Scholarship.

**Skills** : SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy), Power BI, Tableau, Oracle BI, SSRS, SSAS, Crystal Reports, ETL/ELT, Data Warehousing, Data Modeling, and more. There's also a languages section — I speak Azerbaijani, Russian, English, Turkish, and some Swedish.

**Projects** : two featured ones right now:
- *CDIO Methods for Warehouse Layout and SOP Enhancement* — applied the CDIO framework at ReMade in Sweden AB to redesign their warehouse layout and build Standard Operating Procedures that cut down bottlenecks in shelving, picking, and packing.
- *Smart Customer & Buffet Monitoring System* — an IoT/computer vision project for smart monitoring in a buffet setting.

**Volunteer** : I'm involved with Data & AI Stockholm and Data Saturday Oslo, and volunteered at Stockholm Pride 2025.

**Interests**:  a section that's a bit more personal, covering things I care about outside of work and academia.

**Contact** : links to email, LinkedIn, GitHub, and phone. Everything is plain text, no obfuscation.



## Tech stuff

- Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS ,  no React, no build step, nothing to install
- Fully mobile responsive, tested across different screen sizes
- Smooth scroll, fade-in animations on sections, active nav highlighting on scroll
- Hamburger menu for mobile
- Video thumbnails for project demos (with custom fallback cards if the video is private/unlisted)
- Hosted on GitHub Pages , the live site just reads `index.html` from this repo
